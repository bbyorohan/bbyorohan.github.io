This Identification project was one of the most interesting projects I have ever worked on. I was inspired by a more critical way of thinking. I was on a trip in Barcelona and I have met a Turkish Designer. Cem Eskinazi. His work inspired me to harder to analyzie the different ways a brand could come alive incooparating data in it. I did a lot of research to figure out the favorite restrooms of Massart Students. Turns out the tower building was the most popular destination for restrooms. I decided use Glyphs to Identify each restroom in the Tower Building.
