Patico D'estambol was a very fun project to work on. I created my own typeface Patico and used it for this project. I had a lot of fun while developing the menu items na d the branding system. The Restaurant would be a mix of Turkish Jewish Cusine and Mediterranean Cusine. I designed this Restaurant to be in the heart of Istabul. Somewhere between Emirgan and Ortaköy.
